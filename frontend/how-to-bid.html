‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel or retract a bid?

Bids are binding and cannot be retracted. Please bid carefully and only place bids you're committed to honoring.

What happens if I'm outbid?

You'll receive an instant notification. If auto-bid is enabled, it will automatically place a higher bid up to your max limit.

How does auto-bid work?

Auto-bid automatically increases your bid by the minimum increment when outbid, up to your set maximum. It's like having a bidding assistant!

What are the fees?

Buyers pay a small 1% service fee on winning bids. Sellers may have listing fees depending on account type.

How do I contact a seller?

You can view seller information on the auction page. After winning, you'll receive the seller's contact details.

What if I don't receive my item?

Use the Return Request feature in your account. Our support team will help resolve any issues.

How do I change the language?

Click the globe icon (üåê) in the top navigation bar and select your preferred language.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, all transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption. We never store your full payment details.
