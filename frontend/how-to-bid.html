How to Bid

Learn how to participate in auctions and place winning bids on ZUBID. Follow these simple steps to get started.

1

Create Your Account

Start by creating a free account on ZUBID. Click the "Sign Up" button in the navigation bar and fill in your details. You'll need to provide:

  • Username and email address
  • Secure password
  • Personal information for verification
Sign Up Now
2

Browse Auctions

Explore our wide selection of auctions. You can:

  • Browse by category (Electronics, Jewelry, Vehicles, etc.)
  • Search for specific items
  • Filter by price range, status, and more
  • View featured auctions on the homepage
Browse Auctions
3

View Auction Details

Click on any auction to see detailed information:

  • High-quality images and descriptions
  • Current bid and bid increment
  • Time remaining (live countdown)
  • Bid history and bidder information
  • Seller information
4

Place Your Bid

When you're ready to bid:

  • Enter your bid amount (must be at least the minimum bid)
  • Optionally enable auto-bid with a maximum limit
  • Click "Place Bid" to submit
  • Watch real-time updates as others bid

5

Win and Pay

If you win the auction:

  • You'll receive a notification when the auction ends
  • An invoice will be generated automatically
  • Complete payment through your preferred method
  • Arrange for delivery or pickup

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I retract a bid?

Bids are final and cannot be retracted. Please bid carefully and only place bids you're committed to.

What happens if I'm outbid?

You'll receive a notification. If you have auto-bid enabled, it will automatically place a higher bid up to your maximum limit.

How does auto-bid work?

Auto-bid automatically increases your bid when others outbid you, up to your specified maximum amount.

What are the fees?

There's a 1% bid fee on winning bids, plus delivery fees based on your location.
